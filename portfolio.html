Pizza Program 2.0 is a simulated program where users can create a pizza based on their personal choices.
Users can choose to spend which sauce and which toppings they would like to include on their pizza.
Once the toppings are chosen, the program will add the topping onto the pizza, and ask the user if they would like cheese or pepper.
At the end of the game, the program will display a delivery image.







Ping Pong is a game where there are first two paddles on the side and a ball that moves across the screen.
When the ball gets in contact with the paddle, it moves in the other direciton.
On the top of the screen, the game keeps score of how many points each person has, or how many times their opponent has missed.
The first player to 10 points wins the game.







This is an E card that Samuel and I made.
It is an Aladdin themed game. It started out with simple slides where the user can click next to move on.
At the end, you can play a game where you get to click on the stars to acquire more points.
The game is never ending, so they can play forever.







The Scratch program we created was a copy of the game, Flappy Bird.
The pipes on the background have randomized heights, so it changes the locations of the poles.
The bird flies up everytime you click your clicker, and it flies down if you dont click anything.
Everytime the bird passes through the pipes, the score increases by 1.
